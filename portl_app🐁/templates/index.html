How PORTL Works

Conditioned Reinforcer

A click signals to the learner that their behavior was correct and a primary reinforcer is coming.

Unconditioned Reinforcer

Reinforcer blocks (tokens) delivered after the click serve as the primary reinforcer.

Session Structure

Each session is divided into intervals of 10 reinforcers. Teachers record data and revise plans between intervals.

Shaping

Successive approximations toward the target behavior are reinforced, with criteria raised as the learner succeeds.
